======================================
 3DMAD Face Spoofing Database
======================================

The `3DMAD Database <http://www.idiap.ch/dataset/3dmad>`_ for face spoofing consists of video clips of mask attacks.
This database was produced at the `Idiap Research Institute <http://www.idiap.ch>`_, in Switzerland.
